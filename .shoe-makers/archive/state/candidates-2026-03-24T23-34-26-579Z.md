## 2. Make RESOLVED_PATTERN more robust to prevent critique loop false positives
**Type**: bug-fix
**Impact**: medium
**Reasoning**: The `RESOLVED_PATTERN` regex in `src/state/world.ts:103` (`/^## Status\s*\n\s*Resolved\.?\s*$/mi`) only matches "Resolved." exactly. Variants like "Resolved — no action required" or "Resolved. No issues found." fail to match, causing resolved critiques to be counted as unresolved, triggering unnecessary fix-critique cycles. This was experienced firsthand during this shift (critique-073). The pattern should tolerate common suffixes after "Resolved" or at minimum accept "Resolved" as a prefix.
